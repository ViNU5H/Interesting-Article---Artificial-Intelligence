The Rise of Artificial Intelligence in Everyday Life

Published on: June 2026 | Written by: ICT Society

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is no longer just a topic for science fiction movies. Today, it is a real technology that shapes how we live, work, and communicate. From smart assistants on our phones to advanced self-driving cars, AI is rapidly evolving.

How AI Works

At its core, Al works by combining large amounts of data with fast, intelligent processing algorithms. This allows the software to learn automatically from patterns or features in the data. For example, when you watch videos online, AI algorithms analyze your choices to recommend the next video.
